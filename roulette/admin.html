Payout

How to Payout

Straight up

The ball lands on the single number where the bet is placed.
Pays 35 to 1

Split

Double Number
If the bet is placed on two numbers.
Pays 17 to 1

Street

Three Number
If the bet is placed on a row of three.
Pays 11 to 1

Corner Bet

Four Number
If the bet is placed on a square of four numbers.
Pays 8 to 1

Five Number

Only one specific bet: 0-00-1-2-3
Pays 6 to 1

Line

Six Number
If the bet placed on the corner of two rows of three.
Pays 5 to 1

Dozens

Twelve Numbers
If the bet placed on 1st, 2nd, or 3rd dozen.
Pays 2 to 1

Column

Twelve Numbers in a row
If the bet placed on 1st, 2nd, or 3rd row.
Pays 2 to 1

18 numbers

(1-18) OR (19-36)
If the bet placed on either 18 numbers.
Pays 1 to 1

Colors

Red or Black
If the bet placed on either Red or Black.
Pays 1 to 1

Numbers

Even or Odd
If the bet placed on either Even or Odd.
Pays 1 to 1
